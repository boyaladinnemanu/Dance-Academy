How to learn online effectively ?

  • How can you learn art online effectively?
  • Prefer one on one sessions
  • Do ask for theory and..
  • Join under an instructor/institution who have a lesson plan though you are learning an art. So there will be a goal to achieve in every session, and that will help both instructor and the student not to lag the class.
  • Make It a habit to keep a record (written notes ) of every class, so if you forget the step or take a break, this can help you in your further learning. And also not to miss any class. Request for compensation classes if the courses are missed.
  • Do regular practice and try to follow the lesson plan of the mentor. Keep a video recording of your practice session for a self-assessment and keep a graph of your learning.
